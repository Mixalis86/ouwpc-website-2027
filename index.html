Welcome to the Home of Oxford University Water Polo Club

Founded in 1891, OUWPC is one of the university's oldest and most historic sports clubs. The club has a thriving membership base that makes up our four squads that compete in BUCS leagues, and the prestigious annual Varsity match against Cambridge — the longest-running water polo competition in the world, first played in 1891! You can find us at the Rosenblatt Pool in the Iffley Road Sports Centre. You can find more information about us here.
