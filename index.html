Welcome to the Home of Oxford University Water Polo Club

Founded in 1891 and based at the Rosenblatt Pool on Iffley Road, Oxford University Water Polo Club (OUWPC) consists of four squads:

  • M1 (Men's Blues)
  • W1 (Women's Blues)
  • M2
  • W2

Our squads compete in BUCS (British Universities & Colleges Sport) leagues, and every year we contest the Varsity match against Cambridge — the longest-running annual water polo rivalry in the world, first played in 1891.
