Squads

Blues

Men's Blues

Compete in the BUCS Open Premier South League and National Championship. We have recently had members who have played internationally at the national age-group level.

Women's Blues

Compete in the BUCS Female Premier South League and the National Championship, recently winning the league and making the final four in the cup.

Seconds

Men's Seconds

Play in the BUCS Open Midlands Tier 2 League and Conference Cup. The team contains a range of aspiring players and newbies and regularly integrates with the Blues in training and matches.

Women's Seconds

The ever growing squad has yet to be entered into BUCS competitions but trains with the Blues year-round, plays in organised friendlies, and plays in Varsity against Cambridge. The Women's Seconds have been a vital pipe-line of players to the first team, with talented newbies developed at Oxford making-up a proportion of the Women's Blues every year.
